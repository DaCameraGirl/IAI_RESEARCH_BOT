This Study is directed to a device with offset blades to provide improved blending.

 
Engineering Physics Food Technology General Engineering Machinery Mechanical Engineering Industrial & Product Design
 Description
This Study is directed to previously unidentified prior art references, including patent and non-patent literature.

For this Study, we want to see a device with blades used for blending, mixing, and/or chopping the contents of a container, preferably for blending foodstuffs.  We are interested in rechargeable devices for the home, such as blenders or food processors, as well as industrial devices.  The main points we need to see are as follows:

a set of blades or a rotor;
a container;
wherein the blades rotate around a rotational axis and blend the contents of the container
wherein the  rotational axis of the blade is offset from the longitudinal axis of the container body (Please note the offset is from the longitudinal axis of the container, not the longitudinal axis of the base of the device);
wherein the offset is between 5% and 15% of the diameter of the blades.
